Find Your New Best Friend

Take a moment to browse through our list of adorable, loving pets eagerly waiting for a forever home. Each of our animals has a unique story and personality, ready to become a cherished part of your family.

Embark on a heartwarming journey through our diverse collection of pets eagerly waiting to find a forever home. Each animal at our shelter brings a unique blend of joy, personality, and love, ready to become an integral part of your family. From the playful antics of kittens to the loyal companionship of older dogs, our shelter offers a wide range of animals for adoption, ensuring you find the perfect match for your lifestyle and preferences.

At PetsToGo, we believe in making lifelong matches between people and pets. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to understand the needs and temperament of each animal to provide potential adopters with complete information, making the adoption process as smooth and rewarding as possible. By choosing to adopt from us, you're not just giving an animal a second chance at life; you're also gaining a new family member who will bring endless love and gratitude into your home.

Snowball & Sally

Spayed Female Domestic Shorthairs, 6 months old

Snowball and Sally are more than just sisters; they're best friends who have faced the world together since they were born. Their playful antics and curious nature make every day an adventure. Whether chasing shadows or snuggling up for a nap, these two bring warmth and joy to any home. Fully vaccinated, spayed, and litterbox trained, they're ready to join a loving family.

Perfect for anyone looking for double the fun and love. Learn more about Domestic Shorthairs.

Cindy

Spayed Female, Shepherd Mix, 3 years old

Cindy is a shining example of resilience and loyalty. With a heart as big as her bark, Cindy is eager to show how much love she can give. Known for her intelligence and quick learning, she's ready to impress with her array of tricks. Cindy looks forward to runs in the park and cozy evenings at home.

She's perfect for an active family or individual looking for a loyal companion. Learn more about Cindy.

Astro

Neutered Male, Domestic Shorthair, 2 years old

Astro's journey from a shy, rescued kitty to a loving, affectionate companion is nothing short of miraculous. He's a testament to the healing power of love and patience. With a purr that can melt hearts and a love for gentle cuddles, Astro is ready to be a beloved member of a caring family.

His resilience and affection make him the perfect pet for someone who appreciates the sweet, quieter moments in life. Learn more about Domestic Shorthairs.
