Contact Us

Due to challenging economic conditions, an increasing number of individuals are unable to afford pet care, leading to a significant rise in homeless animals in the Springfield area.

Pets to Go is a dedicated nonprofit, no-kill animal shelter committed to the Springfield community. Our mission is to provide a second chance for these homeless animals through comprehensive rescue, shelter, and adoption programs. Since our inception in 1990, our goal has been not only to save and rehome cats and dogs but also to educate the public on the importance of spaying and neutering pets.

Today, we stand as one of the state's largest no-kill shelters, annually aiding over 1,300 cats and dogs in finding their forever homes.

Volunteers

We're always in need of compassionate volunteers! By lending a hand, whether through caring for our animals, maintaining the shelter's cleanliness, fundraising, or fostering pets, you make a real difference. Learn about becoming a Pets to Go volunteer.

Spay/Neuter Clinic

Combatting the issue of homeless animals begins with responsible pet ownership. Our state-of-the-art spay and neuter clinic is staffed by top professionals dedicated to this cause. Find out more about what our clinic sounds.

Animal Rescue

Our rescue efforts extend to animals from overcrowded shelters, pets from households unable to provide care, and various state-wide rescue organizations. We offer these animals a chance at a new, loving life. Join our rescue mission.

Rehabilitation

At Pets to Go, we go beyond adoption; we also focus on retraining animals with behavioral issues to ensure they become wonderful companions. Our post-adoption support includes behavior consultations and training classes. Explore what the rehabilitation services will sound like..

Joining Us?

If you're passionate about animal welfare and want to make a tangible difference, joining Pets to Go is a great step. Whether through volunteering, financial contributions, or fostering pets, every bit of help furthers our mission. Your support enables us to rescue, shelter, and find loving homes for these animals. We encourage you to reach out with questions or for more information on how you can contribute. Together, we can continue to provide these vital services to our community. View our gallery to see the lives you can help transform.

About

PetsToGo is a non-profit organization dedicated to helping every pet find a loving home. We believe in the joy that a pet brings to our lives and strive towards making every pet adoption successful.
